ABOUT

We are a group of foreign residents who are keen to share the beauty of Hirado with others.

Hirado is an island on the far West corner of Kyushu. It's home to gorgeous beaches, mountains, delicious foods, and all that good stuff.

In our group of friends, we have challengers, connoisseurs, active hikers, and photographers ("relaxers").

Each of us picked their favourite spots to share with you. / We hand-selected our favourite spots in Hirado.

Enjoy!
